Buttons

Buttons can be categorized based on form and function. These are three categories for buttons based on function:

1. primary - Ones that call for action and are bold and bright. A page can have just one of these.
2. link - Button that looks like a link
3. passive(default) - Buttons that are used in the page which do not immediately call for action. They are not the primary action for the page.
4. danger - Buttons that are used to indicate operations that can have consequences.
5. nostyle - No styles attached to the button. Relies on the user to set the styles based on parent element.
6. buttons with icons - Buttons with icons to the left and right of the buttons
7. zombie - Buttons that have a dashed border and is typically used with configurations.

All buttons have a minimum width set. They can have two size variations: 

1. small(default)
2. medium (.medium selector)

Pods

Pods are containers for content.

Here are two types of pods.

  1. Default pod - Just a pod with some actions in it
  2. Clickable pod - The entire pod is clickable to take you some where.
  3. Open Pod - If the pod is not clickable and contains information that is large, the pod is open. The content section does not have a box around it.

Layers

Controlling the number of layers a page has with z-index selectors. Use the following selectors to get the specific layer. This eliminates the need to manually set z-index that can be difficult to track.

1. .layer-critical or var(--layer-critical)    gives you a zindex of 10000
2. .layer-high or var(--layer-high)            gives you a zindex of 9000
3. .layer-medium or var(--layer-medium)        gives you  a zindex of  8000
4. .layer-low or var(--layer-low)              gives you a zindex of 7000
5. .layer-backdrop or var(--layer-backdrop)    gives you a zindex of 6000

Spacing

Paddings and Margins for all your elements and components.

Here are the sizes:

xs: 4px
s: 8px
m: 12px
l: 16px
xl: 24px

Here is the structure for selector options

1. .sp-padding-<size>-<location: optional>
   Values for Size: xs, s, m, l, xl
   Values for location: <empty>, top, right, bottom, left, xaxis, yaxis
   Examples: .sp-padding-xs-top, .sp-padding-xs, .sp-padding-l-xaxis 

2. .sp-margin-<size>-<location: optional>
   Values for Size: xs, s, m, l, xl
   Values for location: <empty>, top, right, bottom, left, xaxis, yaxis
   Examples: .sp-margin-xs-top, .sp-margin-xs, .sp-margin-l-xaxis

In addition to regular paddings and margins, supporting spacing options for items within a container. The spacing is applied to every immediate item inside the container except the last.

.sp-group-margin-<size>-<location>

Size can be xs, s, m, l, xl
location can be yaxis or xaxis.
Examples: .sp-group-margin-s-xaxis
